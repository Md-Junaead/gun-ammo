Project ChildSafe

About Project ChildSafe

Project ChildSafe, a program affiliated with the National Shooting Sports Foundation, is deeply devoted to advancing firearm safety and education. Their unwavering dedication to genuine firearm safety is evident in their extensive efforts to provide safety education and distribute free firearm safety kits to communities all across the United States. Thanks to this initiative, local law enforcement agencies have distributed an impressive 40 million gun locks and counting.

Firearms Policy Coalition

About Firearms Policy Coalition

Firearms Policy Coalition is a nonprofit organization tirelessly defending the People’s unrestricted access to and exercise of the Natural Rights necessary for a free world. Their focus encompasses the Right to Keep and Bear Arms and related issues, including freedom of speech, due process, protection against unlawful searches and seizures, separation of powers, asset forfeitures, privacy, encryption, and limited government.

Gunammoshop.com proudly partners with FPC to champion individual, natural, human, and Constitutional rights through their litigation efforts. If you are interested in supporting the fight against government overreach, please consider making a donation.

American Suppressor Association

About American Suppressor Association

The American Suppressor Association has one mission: to advocate for your suppressor rights. They serve as the boots on the ground in the battle to legalize and deregulate suppressors, defending against the anti-suppressor factions that seek to ban them. No other entity has invested more time and resources into suppressors than ASA.

Since its inception in 2011, ASA has played a pivotal role in legalizing suppressor ownership in 3 states, allowing suppressor hunting in 19 states, and thwarting attempts to ban suppressors in 2 states. Their relentless commitment to your Second Amendment right to own and use suppressors remains unwavering.
