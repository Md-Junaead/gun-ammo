LAYAWAY POLICY

// Secure your gear today. Pay over time.

At Gunammoshop.com, we’re pleased to offer our layaway service for in-store purchases. Our layaway policy ensures a convenient and flexible way for you to acquire the products you desire. Here are the details of our layaway policy:

01

Initiation

To start a layaway, we require a modest 10% down payment on each new layaway agreement.

02

Payment Schedule

Your first payment must be made within 30 days of the previous payment to keep your layaway active and ensure your chosen items are reserved for you.

03

Completion

All merchandise placed on layaway must be fully paid for and picked up within 90 days from the original layaway date. This timeframe allows you ample opportunity to make your payments and secure your products.

04

Service Charges

Should you fail to complete your layaway purchase within the 90-day period or choose to cancel the layaway at any time, Gunammoshop.com will apply a service charge of $10.00. This fee covers the handling, storage, and record-keeping expenses associated with the layaway agreement.

05

Firearm Layaways

For firearms placed on layaway, they can be held for a maximum of 60 days. The necessary paperwork should be finalized after the final payment for the firearm has been made. In the event that a customer is not approved to purchase the firearm, Gunammoshop.com will assess a service charge of $10.00.

06

Limitations

Each customer is allowed to put a maximum of 2 guns on layaway. Please note that clearance items are not eligible for layaway and may be purchased through regular means.

07

Policy Adjustments

Gunammoshop.com reserves the right to modify our layaway policy at any time to better serve our customers. We encourage you to check our website or inquire in-store for the most up-to-date information on our layaway terms and conditions.

NEED HELP?

Our layaway service is designed to make your shopping experience more convenient and accessible. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
